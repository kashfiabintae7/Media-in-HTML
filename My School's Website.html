Welcome to Siddheswari Girls High School!

Located in the bustling Baily Road area of Dhaka, Bangladesh, Siddheswari Girls High School has been a cornerstone of education for young women for several decades. Known for it's commitment to academic excellence, the school provides a nurturing environment for girls to thrive both academically and personally.

Campus Overview and Location



Siddheswari Girls High School is strategically located in the heart of Ramna, Dhaka, providing easy access for students across the city. The school was established in 1933. The campus features modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science labs, computer labs, and a library.

The school is set in a spacious, green environment, offering a peaceful atmosphere conducive to both academic and extracurricular activities. Its central location ensures that the school is easily accessible by public transport, making commuting convenient for all students.

Facts and Facilities

  • Total Classrooms:
    The school has a total of 70+ classrooms, each designed to foster a conducive learning environment.

  • Total Students:
    Siddheswari Girls High School currently has a estimated number of 4,000 students, providing education from grade junior one to Grade 10.

  • Total Teachers:
    The school employs more than 150 qualified teachers, each committed to the personal and academic growth of the students.

  • Campus Size:
    The school spans over 3 acres of land with a huge playground right in the middle, providing ample space for outdoor activities and sports.
